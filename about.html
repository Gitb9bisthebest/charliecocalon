Inside my head / slightly filtered

I began my journey in web development with Shopify at a local agency called Blue Ecom Solutions . There, I learned how to edit Shopify code, customize themes, and add sections that made online stores more flexible for our clients.

Along the way, I noticed that many small businesses struggled to continue after Shopify’s $1/month promo ended, since the regular $28/month fee was tough for startups. That inspired me to explore building affordable websites from scratch. I picked up HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, then advanced into frameworks like React.js and Next.js.

Today, I balance both worlds: I still build and customize Shopify stores for businesses that want the convenience of the platform, while also creating custom-coded websites with React and Next.js for clients who need flexibility and scalability.

Whether it’s a Shopify e-commerce store or a full-stack web app, my goal is the same — to deliver websites that are functional, affordable, and built to last.
